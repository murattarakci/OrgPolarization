A quick, confidential map of who you talk to

Three short questions, about three minutes. Your answers are private. No one sees the names you enter. We use them only to show you your own result and to build one anonymous group picture for the debrief.

You can download your own result at the end, with names removed.

Question 1 of 3
Name the people you regularly talk to and trust at work.

Up to five names. First names or initials are fine.

Question 2 of 3
Do these people talk to each other?

For each pair, choose yes or no.

Question 3 of 3
How similar are their political views to yours?

1 = very different  ·  5 = very similar
